**Scope:** entire public site and shared code, excluding `app/admin-dashboard/**` and its server actions (separate pass). Deployment/environment setup and the parked admin access-control decision are out of scope.

## Launch-readiness call

**Not ready for public launch as-is.** The build is healthy (`pnpm build` passes, TypeScript strict, no suppressed errors) and the architecture's core contracts hold (force-dynamic freshness, defensive Supabase fallbacks, RLS read/write boundaries, seed↔migration sync verified byte-identical). But the site currently ships: a catalog where **more than half the products have broken images**, a homepage hero that **rotates third-party brand logos as full-bleed photos**, brand **fonts that never render**, a quote funnel that **throws away every new visitor's selections**, dialogs that fail the **WCAG 2.1 AA standard the footer itself commits to**, and **legal-consent links that go nowhere** while PII is collected.
